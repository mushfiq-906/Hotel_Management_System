HOTEL RAINBOW

ABOUT US

Located in a peaceful area, Hotel Rainbow provides serenity which makes you feel at one with nature. The rooms are modern, spacious, comfortable, super clean, nicely decorated, and they cover all your needs.You can take the advantage of the parking lot, which will make your arrival and departure very easy and comfortable. Staffs are very hospitable. Every room has a corner view and there are lots of windows. The menu in the dining room is innovative and the quality of food is really very good. Check-in is easy and super friendly. It is a well designed futuristic hotel and a very good place to stay in Chittagong.
